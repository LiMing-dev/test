Average Hourly Income for Software Developers in Poland (2023)
*****************************************************************
To estimate the average hourly income for software developers in Poland, we can use typical monthly salary data and adjust for the average number of working hours per month.

Junior Developer (Entry-Level):
--------------------------------------------------
Monthly Salary Range: 7,000–10,000 PLN (~1,650–2,300 USD)
Hourly Rate: 35–50 PLN/hour (~8–11 USD/hour)

Mid-Level Developer (3–5 years of experience):
---------------------------------------------------
Monthly Salary Range: 12,000–18,000 PLN (~2,800–4,100 USD)
Hourly Rate: 60–85 PLN/hour (~13–18 USD/hour)

Senior Developer (5+ years of experience):
---------------------------------------------------
Monthly Salary Range: 18,000–25,000 PLN (~4,100–5,800 USD)
Hourly Rate: 90–120 PLN/hour (~20–26 USD/hour)

Tech Lead/Software Architect:
---------------------------------------------------
Monthly Salary Range: 25,000–35,000 PLN (~5,800–8,100 USD)
Hourly Rate: 120–170 PLN/hour (~26–37 USD/hour)
---------------------------------------------------
Calculation Basis:
Working Hours per Month: Typically around 160 hours (assuming 40 hours per week).
---------------------------------------------------
Summary:
Junior Developer: 8–11 USD/hour
Mid-Level Developer: 13–18 USD/hour
Senior Developer: 20–26 USD/hour
Tech Lead/Software Architect: 26–37 USD/hour
